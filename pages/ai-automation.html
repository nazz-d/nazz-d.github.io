Practical Applications in the Homelab

I have utilized these tools to assist with several high-impact tasks in my own environment:

  • Documentation Audits: Using agents to crawl my homelab-docs repo and identify stale IP addresses or missing device descriptions.
  • SVG Diagram Precision: Leveraging AI to straighten arrows and fix z-order overlaps in my architecture diagrams—tasks that are tedious for humans but perfect for LLMs.
  • Resume Optimization: Building custom scripts with Playwright and AI to measure and export ATS-friendly resumes that fit perfectly on a single page.
